Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-Yoked? +

E-Yoked is an AI-powered matchmaking platform designed to connect startups with strategic partners, fostering collaboration and vertical integration. Our platform helps startups find partners with complementary strengths and shared values to increase their chances of success.

How does E-Yoked work? +

E-Yoked uses advanced algorithms to match startups with potential partners based on their needs, strengths, and values. Users create profiles detailing their business, goals, and requirements. The platform then suggests potential matches for collaboration.

Who can use E-Yoked? +

E-Yoked is designed for startups and small businesses looking for strategic partnerships. It's particularly beneficial for new startups seeking to overcome challenges and scale their operations through collaboration.

What are the benefits of using E-Yoked? +

Increased Success Rates: By finding the right partners, startups can leverage complementary skills and resources. Innovation: Collaborating with other businesses can lead to the development of new and innovative products or services. Sustainability: Partnerships can help startups address sustainability goals and contribute to broader social and economic development. Support and Resources: Access to a network of like-minded businesses provides invaluable support and resources.

How does E-Yoked ensure the quality of matches? +

E-Yoked uses a sophisticated algorithm that considers various factors, including business goals, values, industry, and specific needs. This ensures that the suggested matches are highly compatible and likely to lead to successful partnerships.

Is E-Yoked available globally? +

Currently, E-Yoked focuses on the African market, particularly Kenya. However, we have plans to expand our services to other regions in the future.

How much does it cost to use E-Yoked? +

E-Yoked offers various pricing plans tailored to different business needs. We have a free basic plan for startups just getting started, as well as premium plans that offer more features and personalized support.

How do I sign up for E-Yoked? +

You can sign up for E-Yoked by visiting our website and creating a profile. The process is straightforward and takes just a few minutes.

What industries does E-Yoked cater to? +

E-Yoked caters to a wide range of industries, including technology, healthcare, agriculture, education, and more. Our platform is designed to be versatile and useful for startups in various sectors.

How does E-Yoked handle data security and privacy? +

E-Yoked takes data security and privacy very seriously. We use advanced encryption methods to protect user data and comply with all relevant data protection regulations. Your information is safe with us.

Can E-Yoked help with funding and investment? +

While E-Yoked's primary focus is on facilitating partnerships, we also provide resources and connections that can help startups find funding and investment opportunities through our network.

How does E-Yoked support sustainable development? +

E-Yoked supports sustainable development by promoting partnerships that address social, economic, and environmental challenges. By facilitating collaborations, we help startups contribute to the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

What kind of support does E-Yoked offer to users? +

E-Yoked offers a range of support services, including customer service, partnership guidance, and access to a network of mentors and experts. We aim to provide comprehensive support to help startups succeed.

How can I provide feedback or get in touch with E-Yoked? +

We value your feedback! You can contact us through our website's contact form or email us directly at support@e-yoked.com. We are always looking to improve our services and welcome any suggestions.

Does E-Yoked offer any training or resources for startups? +

Yes, E-Yoked offers a variety of training programs, webinars, and resources to help startups grow and succeed. These resources cover topics like business development, partnership strategies, and market expansion.
